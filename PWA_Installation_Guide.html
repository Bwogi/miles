üçé iOS Installation

Open Safari Browser
Launch Safari on your iPhone or iPad and navigate to your app URL. Important: You must use Safari - other browsers won't work for installation.
Tap the Share Button
Tap the Share button (square with arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the Safari screen.
Select "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the share menu and tap "Add to Home Screen." You may need to scroll to find this option.
Customize App Name
Edit the app name if desired (default is "Vehicle Tracker") and tap "Add" in the top right corner.
Launch the App
Find the "Vehicle Tracker" icon on your home screen and tap it. The app will open in full-screen mode like a native app.
Note: The app icon will appear on your home screen and can be moved to folders or rearranged like any other app.
